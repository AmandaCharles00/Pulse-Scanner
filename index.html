Get Started
You need a Polygon.io Starter API key (~$29/mo) to sync live market data. Your key is stored locally in your browser and never shared.
Step 1 — Enter your API key above and hit Sync
↑ Paste key in the field at top right, then click ⟳ Sync
Step 2 — Explore your themes
After syncing, browse 48 themes ranked by performance. Click any theme to see individual tickers with ATR, volume, and % change across all timeframes.
Step 3 — Set up TradingView (optional)
In TradingView, save your indicators as a Default Chart Layout so they load automatically when you click any ticker link.
